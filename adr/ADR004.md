# ADR 004: Lightweight Email Alerting
## Status

Accepted

## Decision

Use SMTP-based email notifications as the primary alerting mechanism.

## Context

The fraud-review process required immediate visibility of suspicious orders.

The solution needed to be:

- Simple
- Reliable
- Easy to maintain
- Low cost

## Decision Rationale

Email notifications satisfied all operational requirements while avoiding additional infrastructure.

Benefits:

- Broad compatibility
- Minimal maintenance
- Fast implementation
- Immediate visibility for reviewers

## Consequences

### Positive

- Low operational complexity
- Easy integration
- Reliable delivery

### Negative

- Limited workflow automation
- Requires reviewer intervention

## Alternatives Considered

### Dedicated Alerting Platforms

Rejected due to additional complexity and operational overhead.

### Custom Notification Systems

Rejected because they introduced unnecessary maintenance requirements.

## Outcome

SMTP-based notifications provided an effective balance between simplicity and operational usefulness.
