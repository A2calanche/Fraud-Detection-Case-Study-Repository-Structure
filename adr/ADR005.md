# ADR 005: Delta Lake for Alert History
## Status

Accepted

## Decision

Store generated fraud alerts in Delta Lake rather than relying exclusively on notification systems.

## Context

Fraud investigations require historical context.

Review teams frequently need to answer questions such as:

- Has this customer been flagged before?
- Has this address appeared previously?
- Which rules triggered the alert?
- What action was taken?

## Decision Rationale

Delta Lake provides:

- Reliable storage
- Historical traceability
- ACID guarantees
- Analytical capabilities

## Consequences

### Positive

- Centralized alert history
- Improved investigations
- Support for reporting and analytics

### Negative

- Additional storage requirements
- Requires maintenance of historical datasets

## Alternatives Considered

### Email Archive Only

Rejected because notification systems are not designed to serve as historical data repositories.

### Separate Relational Database

Rejected because it introduced additional operational complexity.

## Outcome

Delta Lake became the system of record for fraud-alert history.
