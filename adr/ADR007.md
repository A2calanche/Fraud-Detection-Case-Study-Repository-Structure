# ADR 007: Operational Exclusion Rules
## Status

Accepted

## Decision

Exclude specific operational scenarios from fraud evaluation.

## Context

Not every unusual transaction is suspicious.

Several legitimate business processes consistently generated false positives.

Examples included:

- Store pickups
- Authorized pickup locations
- Assisted sales

## Decision Rationale

Excluding these scenarios improves signal quality and reduces unnecessary investigations.

Typical exclusions include:

### Store Pickup Orders

Orders explicitly marked for pickup.

### Authorized Pickup Locations

Known pickup stands and collection points.

### Assisted Sales

Transactions processed with direct assistance from a sales representative.

## Consequences

### Positive

- Reduced false positives
- Improved analyst efficiency
- Better alert quality

### Negative

- Exclusion lists require maintenance
- Business processes may evolve over time

## Alternatives Considered

### Evaluate Every Order

Rejected because operational exceptions generated excessive review noise.

## Outcome

Operational exclusions became a key component of maintaining a manageable and actionable fraud-review queue.
